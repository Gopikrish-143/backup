WASTE WATER TREATMENT / SEWAGE TREATMENT PLANT

The treatment of wastewater, including sewage, sullage, and kitchen waste, involves a multi-stage process designed to remove sediments, dissolved or suspended organic contaminants, oil, grease, and reduce biochemical oxygen demand (BOD), chemical oxygen demand (COD), and nitrogenous matter to meet the regulatory standards for discharge or reuse.

The main goal of sewage treatment is to ensure that the treated effluent does not pollute the soil or groundwater when discharged back into the environment or that it is safe to recycle for various intended uses. Treated sewage can be repurposed for a wide range of applications in residential areas, commercial establishments, and industrial facilities. Recycling treated sewage has the potential to reduce fresh water consumption by up to 60%.

The size and technology of the sewage treatment plant are determined by factors such as the volume and characteristics of the sewage, required output quality, space availability, site conditions, and the intended uses of the treated water.

Types of Sewage Treatment Technologies and Processes

COEQUAL INFOTECH offers a variety of sewage treatment plant designs, including conventional, modular, packaged, and containerized systems, utilizing several advanced technologies:

✎ Activated Sludge Process (ASP)
✎ Fluidized Bio Bed Reactor (FBBR)
✎ Submerged Aerobic Fixed Film Reactor (SAFF)
✎ Sequential Batch Reactor (SBR)
✎ Membrane Bio Reactor (MBR)

These plants can be installed on the ground, underground, or on elevated platforms, depending on site conditions and user preferences. COEQUAL INFOTECH customizes solutions to meet specific client needs, ensuring efficient wastewater treatment while adhering to environmental standards.

Process Automation and Remote Monitoring

While a manual sewage treatment system can be just as effective as an automated one if operated and maintained properly, process automation plays a crucial role in reducing operational complexity and minimizing human errors. COEQUAL INFOTECH leverages advanced electronics and IT/Cloud-based tools for partial or complete automation and remote monitoring of sewage treatment plants. This integration of technology ensures smooth operation, higher efficiency, and real-time control, reducing the risk of downtime and improving overall system reliability.
