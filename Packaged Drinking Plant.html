PACKAGED DRINKING PLANT

COEQUAL INFOTECH offers advanced Packaged Drinking Water Plants designed to provide pure, safe drinking water in compliance with global standards. We specialize in providing high-quality plants for businesses of all sizes, whether you're looking for small-scale production or large-scale bottled water manufacturing. Our plants are available in Automatic, Semi-Automatic, and Fully Online options, allowing for flexibility and scalability based on your production needs.

At COEQUAL INFOTECH, we specialize in providing advanced Packaged Drinking Water Plants that ensure the highest standards of water purification. Our process starts with Pressure Sand Filtration (PSF), which removes larger suspended solids and particles from raw water, improving its clarity. Next, the water passes through Activated Charcoal Filters (ACF) to eliminate any color, odor, chlorine, and organic contaminants, enhancing both its taste and appearance. The water is then filtered through Micron Filters to remove smaller particles, ensuring high clarity.

The core of our purification process is the Reverse Osmosis (RO) system, which uses a semi-permeable membrane to effectively remove dissolved salts, heavy metals, and other harmful contaminants. Afterward, the water undergoes UV Sterilization, where it is exposed to ultraviolet light to kill any bacteria, viruses, or pathogens. Following this, another round of Micron Filtration ensures the removal of any remaining impurities, and the final stage of Ozonation adds an extra layer of disinfection, ensuring that the water is free from microorganisms and safe for consumption.

Raw Water Testing Equipment

Before initiating the water treatment process, it’s crucial to assess the quality of the raw water. COEQUAL INFOTECH provides Raw Water Test Kits that check essential parameters like pH, Total Dissolved Solids (TDS), hardness, and microbial contamination. These tests help us understand the raw water quality and design the appropriate treatment solution for your specific needs.

Main Purification Stage

The heart of the packaged water plant, Reverse Osmosis (RO), purifies the water by removing dissolved salts, heavy metals, and other harmful contaminants, ensuring the water is safe for consumption. Additionally, UV Sterilizers provide a crucial disinfection process by killing bacteria and viruses, while Ozonators add a further layer of protection, eradicating pathogens and ensuring that the water is completely safe.

Pre-Treatment Stage

In the pre-treatment stage, we ensure that the water is free from large particles and harmful organic contaminants:

✦ Pressure Sand Filter (PSF) is used to remove large suspended solids and particles, enhancing water clarity.
✦ Activated Carbon Filter (ACF) eliminates chlorine, odor, color, and organic impurities, improving the taste and quality of the water.
✦ Micron Filters are used to remove any remaining finer particles, ensuring water clarity before the main purification process begins.

Post-Treatment Stage

After the water undergoes the RO and UV treatment processes, we take additional steps to improve its purity and taste:

✦ Micron Filters are used for final filtration to ensure that the water is completely free from any remaining particles.
✦ Mixed Bed Filters (MB) are used to polish the water by providing additional ion exchange, further enhancing the taste and overall quality of the water.

Quality Control (Lab Equipment)

Maintaining the highest standards of water quality is crucial for packaged drinking water:

✦ COEQUAL INFOTECH provides Lab Equipment for Regular Water Testing to monitor pH, TDS, microbial contamination, and other critical parameters throughout the production process.
✦ Conductivity Meters are used to measure the purity of the water, ensuring that it meets the required standards for packaged drinking water.

Bottling and Packaging

Once the water has been treated and purified, it is ready for packaging:

✦ Jar Washing Machines automatically clean the bottles or jars before the filling process to ensure they are free from contaminants. ✦ Filling Machines precisely fill the bottles or jars with purified water to the desired level, maintaining consistency across batches. ✦ Cap Sealing Machines securely seal the bottles to ensure safety, hygiene, and prevention of contamination.

Labeling and Shrink Wrapping

After filling and sealing, the bottles or jars are ready for branding and packaging:

✦ Sticker Labeling Machines apply high-quality labels to the bottles, ensuring accurate product identification and branding.
✦ Shrink Wrapping Machines wrap the bottles in shrink film to securely pack them for transportation and retail display.
✦ Batch Coding Machines print important information such as batch numbers and expiry dates on the labels, ensuring compliance with regulatory standards and enhancing traceability.
